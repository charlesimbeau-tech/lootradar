Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 17, 2026

Information We Collect

LootRadar does not collect personal information directly. We do not require registration, login, or any form of personal data to use the site.

Analytics

We use GoatCounter for basic, privacy-friendly analytics. GoatCounter does not use cookies, does not collect personal data, and does not track users across websites. We use it solely to understand aggregate traffic patterns.

Advertising

LootRadar may display advertisements from third-party advertising networks. These networks may use cookies or similar technologies to serve relevant ads. You can manage your ad preferences through your browser settings or the ad network's opt-out mechanisms.

Affiliate Links

LootRadar contains affiliate links to game stores. When you click a deal and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission through the CheapShark affiliate program. This does not affect the price you pay.

Third-Party APIs

Game deal data is sourced from the CheapShark API. Your search queries are sent to CheapShark's servers to retrieve deal information. Please refer to CheapShark's website for their privacy practices.

Data Storage

LootRadar is a static website hosted on GitHub Pages. We do not maintain databases or store user data on our servers. Any preferences (such as filter settings) are stored locally in your browser.

Children's Privacy

LootRadar is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at contact@thelootradar.com.
