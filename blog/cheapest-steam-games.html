Cheapest Steam Games Worth Playing in 2025

Last updated: February 2026 · 9 min read · By the LootRadar Team

Steam has over 70,000 games. Most of them are garbage. But buried in the noise are hundreds of incredible games you can buy for less than a cup of coffee. The trick isn't finding cheap games — it's finding cheap games that are actually good.

We've done the digging. Here are the best games on Steam you can grab for under $5 and under $10, plus tips on how to find deals like these consistently using LootRadar.

Best Steam Games Under $5

These regularly drop below $5 during sales, and some sit there permanently. At these prices, they're practically free.

Indie Masterpieces

  • Celeste — Precision platformer with a heartfelt story about anxiety and perseverance. One of the best indie games ever made. Regularly $4.99 or less on sale.
  • Hollow Knight — 40+ hours of gorgeous Metroidvania exploration. The fact that this costs less than a sandwich during sales is criminal.
  • Undertale — The RPG where you don't have to kill anyone. Wildly creative, deeply moving, and dirt cheap.
  • Stardew Valley — Farming sim perfection. Hundreds of hours of content for under $5 on sale. One of the best value propositions in gaming.
  • Terraria — 2D sandbox with absurd depth. The developers have released massive free updates for over a decade. Currently sitting at Overwhelmingly Positive reviews.

Action & Roguelikes

  • Hades — Supergiant's roguelike masterpiece hits $5 during major sales. Tight combat, incredible story integration, endlessly replayable.
  • Dead Cells — Fast, brutal roguevania with buttery-smooth combat. Dozens of free content updates since launch.
  • Vampire Survivors — The game that spawned an entire genre. $2.99 at full price. On sale, it's basically free.
  • Slay the Spire — Deckbuilding roguelike perfection. Simple to learn, impossible to master, $4.99 or under on sale.

Strategy Gems

  • Into the Breach — Turn-based mech tactics from the FTL devs. Perfect information, zero randomness, pure strategy.
  • FTL: Faster Than Light — Manage a spaceship and crew across a desperate journey. A decade old and still unmatched.
  • Civilization V — The complete edition regularly drops under $5. That's hundreds of hours of "one more turn."

Best Steam Games Under $10

Stretch your budget to $10 and the options get ridiculous. These are games that competed for Game of the Year awards — now available for pocket change.

AAA Games That Drop Low

  • The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt — One of the greatest RPGs ever made. GOTY edition with both expansions regularly hits $9.99. Over 100 hours of content.
  • Doom (2016) — The shooter revival that reminded everyone how fun pure action can be. $5.99 on sale.
  • Dishonored — Immersive sim brilliance. Stealth, powers, multiple paths through every level. Definitive Edition goes under $10 regularly.
  • Bioshock Collection — Three legendary games in one package. Under $10 during major sales.
  • Portal Bundle — Both Portal games for under $3 on sale. If you haven't played these, stop reading and go buy them.

Hidden Gems Most People Miss

  • Outer Wilds — A solar system stuck in a 22-minute time loop. One of the most unique games ever made. Goes under $10 on deep sales.
  • Disco Elysium — An RPG where your skills are aspects of your personality and they argue with each other. Revolutionary writing.
  • Inscryption — Starts as a card game, becomes something else entirely. The less you know going in, the better.
  • Subnautica — Open-world underwater survival that's equal parts beautiful and terrifying. Under $10 on sale.
  • Rimworld — Colony sim and story generator. Rarely goes below $10, but when it does, it's an instant buy.

How to Find the Best Steam Deals

Steam sales happen regularly, but the best deals aren't always obvious. Here's how to consistently find the cheapest prices:

Know the Sale Calendar

Steam runs major sales roughly every quarter: the Summer Sale (June), Autumn Sale (November), Winter Sale (December), and Spring Sale (March). But publishers also run their own sales constantly. Check our Ultimate Steam Sale Guide for exact dates and strategies.

Don't Just Check Steam

Here's a secret many gamers miss: Steam keys are sold by dozens of authorized retailers, and they often beat Steam's own prices. Stores like Fanatical, Green Man Gaming, Humble Bundle, and GamersGate sell legitimate Steam keys — sometimes 10-20% cheaper than Steam's own discounts.

Checking all these stores manually is tedious. That's exactly the problem LootRadar solves — it aggregates deals from 15+ stores in real time so you can see the absolute lowest price for any game across every legitimate retailer.

Use Price History

A game that's "50% off" might still be overpriced if it was never worth the original asking price. Always check whether the current sale price matches or beats the historical low. LootRadar shows you deal ratings based on price history so you can tell at a glance whether a discount is genuinely good.

Wishlist Strategically

Add games to your Steam wishlist and you'll get email notifications when they go on sale. But combine this with LootRadar to catch deals from non-Steam stores that wishlist notifications won't cover.

Budget Gaming Is the Smart Way to Game

There's a certain satisfaction in paying $3 for a game that someone else paid $60 for at launch — and having the exact same experience. Patient gaming isn't about missing out. It's about getting more for less.
